# Steelman

This chapter describes steelmanning in STE: articulating the strongest version of a proposal or counter-argument to improve decision quality. It distinguishes good-faith strengthening from rhetorical games.

## Why this matters

Steelmanning complements interrogation by reducing strawman failures in architecture debate.
