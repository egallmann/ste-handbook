# Semantic Graphs

This chapter explores semantic graph representations beyond basic IR graphs: ontologies, reasoning, and interoperability considerations. It signals research and engineering frontiers without fixing a single approach.

## Why this matters

Semantic richness can improve automation but increases complexity. This chapter prepares readers for trade-offs.
