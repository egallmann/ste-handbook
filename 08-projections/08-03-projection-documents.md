# Projection Documents

This chapter covers documents generated from IR and intent: architecture descriptions, decision summaries, and certification packets. It emphasizes structure and trace anchors over prose quality alone.

## Why this matters

Documents are delivery vehicles for governance. Their structure should reflect traceable sources.
