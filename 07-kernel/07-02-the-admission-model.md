# The Admission Model

This chapter explains admission: which artifacts enter the reasoning graph, under what validity conditions, and how stale or incompatible inputs are rejected or quarantined. It connects compilation errors to operational policy.

## Why this matters

Admission is the security and integrity perimeter for automated reasoning. Weak admission undermines trust in assessments.
