# Convergence (Kernel)

This chapter discusses convergence from the kernel’s perspective: processes and mechanisms that reduce divergence through targeted changes, new evidence, or revised intent. It complements—but does not duplicate—lifecycle convergence language.

## Why this matters

Kernel-centric convergence explains what automated assessment expects to see as systems move toward alignment.
