# Deterministic Assessment

This chapter focuses on assessments that are repeatable under fixed inputs: why determinism matters, what kinds of STE checks aim for it, and where non-determinism must be explicitly bounded.

## Why this matters

Deterministic assessment supports auditability and automation. This chapter sets expectations without overpromising.
