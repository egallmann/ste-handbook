# Part 7 — Kernel overview

This chapter introduces the STE kernel as the reasoning and validation engine over IR and evidence: admission, validation, divergence and convergence concepts, runtime evidence ingestion, conformance, and deterministic assessment framing.

At handbook altitude, the **Kernel** role is where **Architecture IR**, **evidence**, and **rules** are orchestrated into **assessment** outcomes for **governance**: the locus that asks, for a declared **scope**, whether **embodiment** matches maintained commitments—not a replacement for human judgment on every ambiguous claim.

## Why this matters

The kernel is where intent meets reality in STE’s story. Readers need a map before individual mechanism chapters.
