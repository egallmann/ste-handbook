# Part 12 — Adoption Overview

This part is reserved for how organizations introduce STE: sequencing, roles, maturity, and common failure modes when scaling from pilots to sustained operation.

## Why this matters

The technical model does not deploy itself. Adoption mechanics determine whether STE stays a handbook exercise or becomes operational practice.

## Planned coverage

- Rollout patterns and prerequisites
- Organizational readiness and training
- Metrics for healthy STE operation
