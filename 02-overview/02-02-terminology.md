# Terminology

This chapter lists and disambiguates core terms used across the handbook: intent, Architecture IR, kernel, projection, evidence, conformance, drift, certification, and governance. It favors consistency over exhaustive glossary.

## Why this matters

Shared vocabulary reduces contradictory readings of later chapters, especially where terms overlap with everyday usage.
