# Kernel

This chapter describes the kernel’s role end-to-end: orchestrating checks, consuming compiled IR, coordinating evidence, and emitting assessments consumable by humans and automation. Implementation repositories are referenced, not specified here.

## Why this matters

Confusion between kernel and “the runtime” or “the agents” blurs accountability. This chapter draws boundaries.
