# Validation

This chapter covers validation rules over IR and intent: structural validity, semantic checks, and invariant enforcement before and during assessment. It distinguishes validation from conformance testing where STE makes that distinction.

## Why this matters

Validation is the gate for meaningful conformance. This chapter clarifies rule classes and outcomes.
