# Decision to Change

This chapter follows approved decisions into concrete changes: patches, migrations, intent updates, and re-certification triggers. It closes the loop back toward implementation.

## Why this matters

Change execution without loop closure recreates drift. This chapter emphasizes feedback completion.

- Change planning hooks
- Re-running compilation and checks
