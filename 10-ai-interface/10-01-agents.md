# Agents

This chapter surveys agent roles in STE conversation and automation: bounded autonomy, tool use, and oversight. It emphasizes policy, logging, and admission rather than hype.

## Why this matters

Agents are force multipliers with risk. STE needs a disciplined framing aligned with kernel governance.
