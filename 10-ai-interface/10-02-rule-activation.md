# Rule Activation

This chapter explains when rules, checks, and prompts activate during conversations and downstream compilation. It connects conversational steering to validation rules without defining a specific rules engine here.

## Why this matters

Uncontrolled rule activation creates noise; too little creates gaps. This chapter frames activation concepts.
