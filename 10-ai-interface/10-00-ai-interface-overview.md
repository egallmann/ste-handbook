# Part 10 — AI interface overview

This part covers STE surfaces where automated agents, rule activation, and machine-mediated assistance connect to governed intent, Architecture IR, and evidence. It complements the human-facing interface in Part 9.

## Why this matters

Without a dedicated place for AI-shaped interfaces, teams mix conversational tooling with policy in ways that are hard to review or bound.
