# Information Theory

This chapter connects information, entropy, and channel concepts to documentation and evidence: what is preserved, what is lost, and why structured representations matter. It supports the lossy-reasoning framing without heavy formalism.

## Why this matters

Information-theoretic intuition helps explain why STE invests in canonical models and traceability rather than unconstrained narrative.
