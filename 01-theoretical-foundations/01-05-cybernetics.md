# Cybernetics

This chapter introduces cybernetic themes—governance, feedback, and steering of complex systems—as historical and conceptual context for STE’s operational model. It complements control theory and lifecycle chapters.

## Why this matters

Cybernetics emphasizes regulation and adaptation; STE’s governance and certification language resonates with that tradition.

## Planned coverage

- Second-order effects: governing the governance process
- Connections to human-in-the-loop operation
