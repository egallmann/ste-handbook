# Control Theory

This chapter presents control ideas—reference signals, feedback, stability, and error signals—as analogies for STE’s continuous certification loop. It avoids implying STE implementations are literal control systems unless specified elsewhere.

## Why this matters

The control-loop part of the handbook borrows vocabulary deliberately. This chapter sets expectations for rigor versus analogy.
