# Safety and Constraints Engineering

This chapter introduces safety and constraint thinking—hazards, invariants, assurance—as they relate to architectural intent and validation. It prepares readers for invariants, constraints, and conformance discussions.

## Why this matters

Many STE systems care about non-functional guarantees. This chapter grounds those concerns in established engineering practice.

- Hazards, mitigations, and assurance concepts (high level)
- Invariants and enforced constraints
- Evidence expectations (preview)
