# Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE)

This chapter relates MBSE practices—canonical models, traceability, lifecycle integration—to STE without equating them. It clarifies what STE adopts, adapts, or leaves to other standards.

## Why this matters

MBSE is a frequent reference point for readers; explicit comparison reduces misunderstanding.
