# Systems Theory

This chapter introduces systems, boundaries, environment, state, and feedback at a level sufficient for later architecture modeling and control-loop language. It stays conceptual and links forward to Architecture IR entities and relationships.

## Why this matters

Without systems vocabulary, discussions of “the system model” become ambiguous. This chapter aligns terms with how the handbook uses them.

## Planned coverage

- System boundary and environment
- Structure, behavior, and emergence (high level)
- Composition and decomposition
