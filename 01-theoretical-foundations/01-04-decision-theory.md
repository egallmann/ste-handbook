# Decision Theory

This chapter outlines decisions under uncertainty, preferences, and evidence at a lightweight level. It foreshadows decision capture, ADR surfaces, and assessment outcomes without prescribing a formal decision calculus.

## Why this matters

STE treats decisions as objects with lifecycle. Decision-theoretic language helps separate options, commitments, and evidence.

## Planned coverage

- Options, commitments, and revisiting decisions
- Evidence’s role in choice (conceptual)
- Interfaces to ADR artifacts
