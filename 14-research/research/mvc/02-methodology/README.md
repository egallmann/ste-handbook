# MVC Methodology Publications

## The Problem

This directory publishes methodology-level MVC research artifacts. Methodology publications define how MVC claims are tested and interpreted.

## The Reframe

Methodology publications are historical research records. They should make changes visible so findings and reproductions can cite the method they used.

## The Model

- Published research artifacts are historical records.
- Minor corrections may be applied in place.
- Substantive revisions create a new publication version.
- Prior versions remain discoverable and referenceable.
- Findings should identify the methodology version they were derived from.

Preferred naming for substantive versions:

- `mvc-methodology-v1.md`
- `mvc-methodology-v2.md`

## Relationship to STE system

Methodology publications define research method, not Runtime behavior or STE contracts.

## Summary

- Methodology publications preserve how claims are tested.
- Substantive revisions create new publication versions.
- Findings and reproductions cite methodology versions.
