# MVC Reproductions

## The Problem

This directory records MVC reproduction attempts. Reproduction quality is a distinct research signal.

## The Reframe

Reproductions must identify what they attempted to reproduce, under which research configuration, and with what status.

## The Model

| Category | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| Internal Reproduction | Reproduction by the original research team or same project context. |
| External Reproduction | Reproduction by a different team with access to provided materials. |
| Independent Reproduction | Reproduction by an independent party using the published package or equivalent declared inputs. |
| Failed Reproduction | A reproduction attempt that does not reproduce the finding or cannot complete under the declared configuration. |

## The Implications

- Reproduction status must be explicit.
- Failed reproductions remain part of the research record.
- Reproductions must identify the research configuration being reproduced.
- Reproductions should identify the finding and publication version under test.

## Relationship to STE system

Reproductions strengthen or challenge research maturity. They do not become normative STE authority.
