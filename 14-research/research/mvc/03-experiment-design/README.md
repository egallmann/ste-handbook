# MVC Experiment Design

## The Problem

This directory publishes experiment-design artifacts for MVC research. Experiment designs instantiate the thesis and methodology under declared research configurations.

## The Reframe

Experiment design is the bridge between methodology and evidence generation. It names the study boundary without embedding operational harness internals.

## The Model

- [MVC experimental design](mvc-experimental-design.md)

## The Implications

Experiment designs are research artifacts. They do not define production Runtime behavior, Kernel admission, benchmark authority, or normative STE contracts.

## Relationship to STE system

Experiment design connects MVC methodology to evidence and reproducibility without changing authority.

## Summary

- Experiment designs instantiate methodology.
