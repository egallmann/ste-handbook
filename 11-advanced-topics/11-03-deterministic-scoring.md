# Deterministic Scoring

This chapter examines scoring methodologies for assessments that must be repeatable and auditable: aggregation functions, weighting, and transparency requirements. It extends deterministic assessment conceptually.

## Why this matters

Scoring is politically sensitive; determinism and explainability reduce disputes.
