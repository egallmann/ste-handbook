# Embodied Decision Records

This chapter discusses decision records embedded in code, configuration, and runtime policy as first-class artifacts. It examines benefits and hazards of “living decisions.”

## Why this matters

Embodiment tightens feedback loops but complicates traceability if unmanaged.
