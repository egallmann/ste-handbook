# Runtime Evidence

This chapter describes runtime evidence types, collection assumptions, and how evidence binds to IR elements and traces. It avoids vendor-specific stacks unless used as examples.

**Evidence** is how **embodiment** becomes comparable to **intent** and **Architecture IR** under **rules**: observations with provenance, tied to **scopes** that IR identities anchor. Model-based and human analysis both depend on that grounding when **stochastic** behavior is in play.

## Why this matters

Conformance without evidence is hollow. This chapter grounds assessment in observable signals.
