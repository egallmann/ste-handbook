# Part 10 — Lifecycle and Governance Overview

This chapter introduces operational models for design, change, drift, convergence, certification, and governance within STE. It connects lifecycle language to the control loop and kernel outputs. For the staged walkthrough of lifecycle phases (intent through change and state), read Part 5 first ([STE lifecycle overview](../05-ste-lifecycle/05-00-lifecycle-overview.md)); this part emphasizes policy and operations that attach to those phases.

## Why this matters

Operations determine whether STE remains a paper architecture. This part grounds the thesis in practice.
