# Part 8 — Conversation Engine Overview

This chapter introduces the conversation engine as STE’s human-facing interface layer: interrogation, decision capture, steelmanning, reference patterns, agents, and human-in-the-loop operation. It explains how conversation interfaces relate to intent and IR.

## Why this matters

Most intent originates in human discussion. Without an interface model, tooling fragments and records diverge from reality.
