# Human-in-the-Loop

This chapter specifies human-in-the-loop expectations: approvals, escalations, accountability, and pairing with automated suggestions. It complements governance and safety discussions.

## Why this matters

Automation without human accountability fails organizations. This chapter states STE’s default stance.
