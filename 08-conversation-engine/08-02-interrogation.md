# Interrogation

This chapter explains interrogation as structured questioning to surface assumptions, risks, and missing decisions. It connects to steelmanning and reference architectures as complementary techniques.

## Why this matters

Poor interrogation produces hollow ADRs. This chapter frames disciplined inquiry as part of STE.
