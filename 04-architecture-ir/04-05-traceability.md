# Traceability

This chapter defines traceability links across intent, IR, implementation artifacts, and evidence. It explains why traces are first-class and how they support certification and audit narratives.

## Why this matters

Without traceability, conformance becomes anecdotal. This chapter frames links as durable engineering data.
