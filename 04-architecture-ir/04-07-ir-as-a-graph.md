# IR as a Graph

This chapter presents Architecture IR as a graph model: queries, paths, and analytical perspectives. It foreshadows advanced graph topics without requiring graph theory prerequisites.

## Why this matters

Graph thinking clarifies traceability, projections, and tooling strategies for IR consumers.

## Planned coverage

- Graph representations and queries (conceptual)
- Analytical patterns
- Limits and performance considerations (high level)
