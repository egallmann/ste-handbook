# Part 4 — Architecture IR Overview

This chapter introduces Architecture IR as the canonical, machine-oriented system model compiled from intent and related inputs. It explains why IR exists, what it is not, and how projections and kernel logic depend on it.

## Why this matters

IR is the hub between human-authored intent and automated assessment. Misunderstanding IR’s role causes tool and process mistakes.
