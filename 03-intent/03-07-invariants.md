# Invariants

This chapter explains invariants as properties that must hold across allowed states and changes. It distinguishes invariants from constraints and capabilities in STE’s intent language at a handbook level.

## Why this matters

Invariant violations are central to validation stories. Readers need a crisp notion before kernel chapters.
