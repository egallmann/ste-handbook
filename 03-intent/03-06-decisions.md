# Decisions

This chapter generalizes decision objects beyond a single ADR format: lifecycle states, supersession, and decision graphs. It supports governance and change-management chapters.

## Why this matters

Decision graphs underpin drift analysis and certification. This chapter sets expectations for how decisions evolve.
