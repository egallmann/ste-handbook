# Architecture Decisions

This chapter explains architecture decisions as durable records: context, options, decision, and consequences. It connects human deliberation to machine-consumable structure where STE requires it.

## Why this matters

Decisions are the atomic unit of many governance workflows. This chapter frames them for ADR-L/PS/PC chapters.
