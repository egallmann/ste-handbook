# Capabilities

This chapter describes capabilities as statements of what the system must be able to do or support, often linked to decisions and evidence. It clarifies how capabilities relate to requirements-style thinking without renaming STE into a requirements methodology.

## Why this matters

Capabilities connect stakeholder language to architectural decisions and tests. They help structure examples and projections.
