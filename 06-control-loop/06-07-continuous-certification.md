# Continuous Certification

This chapter defines continuous certification as repeated loop execution with explicit policies for frequency, scope, and escalation. It relates to lifecycle certification language without duplicating all policy detail.

## Why this matters

Certification as a batch artifact alone misses operational reality. Continuous framing matches STE’s thesis.
