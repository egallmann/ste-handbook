# Assessment to Decision

This chapter describes decision-making triggered by assessments: approving changes, opening exceptions, scheduling remediation, or revising intent. It ties kernel outputs to governance.

## Why this matters

STE assumes decisions are explicit and recorded. This chapter connects assessments to durable outcomes.
