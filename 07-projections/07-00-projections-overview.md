# Part 7 — Projections Overview

This chapter introduces projections: derived views from Architecture IR and related artifacts for humans and tools. It explains why multiple projections coexist and how consistency is maintained conceptually.

## Why this matters

Projections are how most stakeholders consume STE. Without a clear model, diagrams and documents contradict IR silently.
