# Stakeholder Views

This chapter addresses projections tailored to non-implementer stakeholders: risk, compliance, product, and operations perspectives. It links capabilities and decisions to readable narratives.

## Why this matters

STE fails in organizations if stakeholders cannot navigate outputs. This chapter sets expectations for accessibility.
