# Intent versus Implementation

This chapter separates what a system is meant to be from how it is realized in repositories and runtime. It explains why both need aligned representations and why conflating them obscures drift and conformance questions.

## Why this matters

STE’s control loop depends on comparing intent-shaped artifacts to implementation-shaped evidence. Confusing the two categories makes assessment ambiguous.
