# Governed Reasoning

This chapter introduces reasoning that operates under explicit rules, scopes, and evidence expectations—contrasted with ad hoc argumentation. It foreshadows the kernel, admission model, and governance parts of the handbook.

## Why this matters

Governance without a model of reasoning devolves into opinion. STE ties reasoning to checkable artifacts and processes described later.
