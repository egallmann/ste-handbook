# Architecture as a First-Class Artifact

This chapter argues for treating architecture as a maintained artifact with structure, versioning, and projections—not only as incidental diagrams or meeting notes. It sets expectations for Architecture IR and projections later in the book.

## Why this matters

If architecture is not an artifact, it cannot be diffed, validated, or governed systematically. This chapter states the design stance STE builds on.
