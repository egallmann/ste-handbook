# The STE Thesis

This chapter states the handbook-level thesis: architectural intent can be captured in structured form, compiled into a canonical model, and subjected to continuous conformance assessment against evidence—reducing uncontrolled drift and lossy reasoning.

## Why this matters

The thesis is the through-line for Parts 3–9. Readers should finish Part 0 knowing what success looks like for STE as a discipline.
