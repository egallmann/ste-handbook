# The Problem of Lossy Reasoning

This chapter describes how rationale degrades across people, time, and artifacts: informal explanations, partial memory, and unstructured documents fail to preserve the structure needed for consistent review or automated checking.

## Why this matters

Lossy reasoning is the practical obstacle STE addresses. Naming the failure mode clarifies what “governed reasoning” is meant to improve, without promising perfection.
