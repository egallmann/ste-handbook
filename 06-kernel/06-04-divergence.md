# Divergence

This chapter explains divergence: detected mismatch between intent or IR expectations and implementation or evidence. It frames divergence signals as inputs to decisions, not moral failures.

## Why this matters

Divergence language appears in drift and certification chapters. A precise definition prevents confusion with generic bugs.
