# Relationships

This chapter covers directed and undirected relationships, dependency, composition, data flow, and deployment mapping in IR. It explains how relationships enable graph queries and impact analysis.

## Why this matters

Architecture is largely relational. Relationship semantics determine valid analyses and meaningful diffs.
